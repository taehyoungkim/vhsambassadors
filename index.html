About Us

As AP Ambassadors, we're all about helping you out– be it through tutoring, mentorship, or everyday management advice. The mission of the AP Ambassador Program is to enhance the environment and academic performance & understanding of every student who asks for help, and to aid in students' adjustment and comprehension of their work.
